What is Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis?

'Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis' is the task of determining the sentiment of a text with respect to a specific aspect. Aspect-based sentiment analysis is a relatively new field of research that has emerged in response to the limitations of traditional sentiment analysis methods.
